The C# Mega Man Engine was crafted from the ground up to allow Mega Man fans to craft high-quality fan games with ease. The goal of the project is to faithfully recreate all of the feel and features of the classic NES series. The project has come a long way, and is still under active development.

Recreate your favorite or most feared stages from the classic series. Customize your tiles, enemies, and more.
